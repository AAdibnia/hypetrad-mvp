HypeTrade Product Roadmap

Turn market hype into your strategic trades.

Our Primary Goal

To help retail traders cut through market hype by documenting the "why" behind every decision, analyzing their patterns, understanding how hype influences their choices, and building a disciplined strategy that works.

Who We're Building For

  • The Aspiring Trader: Eager to start but needs to learn emotional control.
  • The Retail Trader: Actively trading but wants to find and fix hidden biases in their strategy.

Known Dependencies & Risks

  • API Access (Phase 1 & Beyond): Our ability to provide real-time, accurate market data is dependent on reliable and affordable API access from data providers.
  • User Adoption & Data Volume (Phase 2 & 3): The effectiveness of our analytics and community features relies on achieving a critical mass of active users who consistently log their trades. Low adoption is a key risk.
  • AI Accuracy (Phase 4): The quality of our AI-powered tips is directly dependent on the volume and quality of the trade data collected. Insufficient data will limit the feature's effectiveness.
